First observations

See am_dl_3_50_25.txt and slurm-1218768.out

  • Converges when close enough (as in AGMM15). For this, even 0.5 is close enough. Note it doesnâ€™t converge to \(A\) - it converges to something that has columns \(\approx 0.1\) away from \(A\), consistant bias. (This makes sense.)
